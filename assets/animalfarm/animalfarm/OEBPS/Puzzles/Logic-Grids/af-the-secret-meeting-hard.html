Animal Farm: The Secret Meeting

Late one evening on Animal Farm, five characters are preparing for a crucial secret meeting. Unravel who is doing what, where, and when.

Clues:

  1. The one organizing documents did so an hour before Clover, who didn't use the binoculars.
  2. Boxer, who didn't gather intelligence, was busy at 9 PM.
  3. The meeting area was prepared with a hammer, not in the barn, but in the location where activities took place at Midnight.
  4. Napoleon was neither in the orchard nor wielding the pen.
  5. The character in the windmill used the lantern, but not for writing speeches.
  6. Guarding the perimeter happened an hour after Squealer's task but was not the last activity of the evening.
